About the Conference
A Premier IEEE Platform for
Power & Renewable Energy
IPRECON 2026 is the 4th edition of India's first and only IEEE Club of 6 Technical Paper Conference, organized by the IEEE Student Branch of the College of Engineering Karunagappally. With technical co-sponsorship from IEEE IAS and IEEE PES, it serves as a premier platform for students, researchers, and professionals to share cutting-edge innovations.

This edition revolves around the theme: "Advancing Power and Renewable Energy Systems through AI, Computing, and Emerging Technologies." All accepted papers are submitted to the IEEE Xplore® Digital Library. Up to 20% of presented papers are eligible for further review and possible publication in IAS Periodicals.
