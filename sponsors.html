About IPRECON 2026

IPRECON 2026 is a premier IEEE-sponsored international conference on power systems, renewable energy, and sustainable technologies, hosted at the College of Engineering Karunagappally, Kerala. As India’s First and Only IEEE Club of 6 Technical Paper Conference, it unites ten IEEE Technical Societies under one roof.

Partnering with IPRECON 2026 connects your organization with a global community of researchers, engineers, and industry professionals - aligning your brand with innovation, academic excellence, and the trusted IEEE name.

IPRECON 2026 is part of the prestigious “Club of Six” IEEE IAS/PES international conferences in India.

Why Sponsor?

  • 300+ international researchers & engineers
  • Peer-reviewed IEEE conference proceedings
  • Global industry-academia network
  • 3-day premier technical conference
